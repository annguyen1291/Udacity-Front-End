Monkey

Monkeys are generally considered to be intelligent, particularly Old World monkeys.

  • Scientific Name: Haplorhine
  • Average Length: 111 centimeters
  • Average Lifespan: 23 years
  • Habitat: Tropical forests

Many monkey species are tree-dwelling (arboreal), although there are species that live primarily on the ground, such as baboons. Most species are also active during the day (diurnal).
